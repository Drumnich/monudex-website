How it works

From wanderlust to a world you own

MonuDex is a real-world collection game for travelers. Discover a landmark, prove you were really there, and watch your atlas of the planet fill in — one monument at a time.

Start collecting
01

Discover what's around you

Open the globe or your nearby list. Every one of the 585 monuments is a real, curated place — vetted for travelers, with accurate location and rich detail.

02

Travel there & verify

Once you're within about 500m, capture a selfie. MonuDex confirms the landmark is actually in frame before it counts — so your collection is always earned.

03

Collect, level up & complete

The card unlocks with its real photo, facts and rarity. Climb streaks and badges, and complete whole countries and continents on your way to the full Dex.

Anti-cheat

A collection you can't fake

Verification combines three signals so every unlock is genuine — your collection means something because it can't be gamed.

On-location GPS
You must physically be within range of the monument — no checking in from the couch.
Landmark in frame
On-device vision checks the actual monument appears in your selfie before it unlocks.
Live capture only
Photos are taken live in-app — uploads from the camera roll don't count.
Privacy first

Your journey, kept yours

Selfies stay private
Your verification photos are never shared or sold, and never appear publicly.
No background tracking
Location is read only when you verify a visit — never silently in the background.
You own your atlas
Export or delete your collection any time. It's your travel story to keep.
On-device where possible
Image checks run on your phone, so raw photos don't need to leave your device.

Legal

Privacy Policy

Last updated June 2026 · MonuDex respects your journey and your data.

1. The short version

MonuDex is built to be private by default. We collect only what's needed to verify a real visit and run your collection. We never sell your data, never share your selfies, and never track your location in the background.

2. What we collect

An account (email or sign-in provider); your collection progress and badges; coarse location only at the moment you verify a visit; and verification selfies. We also keep basic, anonymised usage analytics to improve the app.

3. How we use it

Location and selfie checks confirm a landmark was really in frame so unlocks are genuine. Your progress powers your atlas, stats and streaks. That's it — no advertising profiles.

4. What we never do

We never sell or rent your personal data; never publish or share your verification photos; and never read your location while the app is closed. Image checks run on-device wherever possible, so raw photos don't need to leave your phone.

5. Your controls

You can export your full collection or delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings. Deletion is permanent and processed within 30 days.

6. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach us at our contact page or privacy@monudex.app.

Terms of Service

Last updated June 2026 · Please read these terms before collecting.

1. Acceptance

By creating a MonuDex account or using the app, you agree to these terms. If you don't agree, please don't use MonuDex.

2. The service

MonuDex is a real-world collection game: you discover monuments, verify visits with a selfie, and build a personal collection with rarities and badges. Features and the monument catalogue may evolve over time.

3. Fair play

Unlocks must be earned by genuinely visiting a monument. Spoofing location, submitting photos you didn't capture on-site, or otherwise circumventing verification is prohibited and may result in removed unlocks or account suspension.

4. Your content

You keep ownership of the selfies and content you create. You grant MonuDex only the limited permission needed to run verification and display your own collection back to you.

5. Safety & conduct

Always follow local laws, site rules, and common sense when visiting monuments. Never trespass or put yourself in danger for a collection — no landmark is worth it.

6. Changes & contact

We may update these terms; we'll flag material changes in-app. Questions? Visit our contact page or email legal@monudex.app.
