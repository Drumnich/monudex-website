How it works

From wanderlust to a world you own

MonuDex is a real-world collection game for travelers. Discover a landmark, prove you were really there, and watch your personal atlas of the planet fill in — one monument at a time.

Start collecting
The loop

Visit. Verify. Collect.

1 · Discover

Open the globe or your nearby list. Each of the 585 monuments is a real, visitable place — curated, not random.

2 · Verify

Travel there. Once you're within range, capture a selfie and the app confirms the landmark is actually in frame.

3 · Collect

The card unlocks with the real photo, facts, and rarity. Your atlas, streaks, and badges grow with every visit.
